Hoary

Definition:

• gray or white with or as if with age

• extremely old : ancient

Pronunciation:

ˈhɔɹi

Etymology:

1510s, "gray or white with age" (of hair); c. 1600 as "venerable, ancient;" from hoar + -y (2). Related: Hoariness.
